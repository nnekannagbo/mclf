Call for collaborators:
Making video meetings weirder

Are you interested in doing experimental and wonderful things on video-conference-calls? Ready to leap into a video call and rescue people from endless tedium and dread? Have ideas on how to make video calls stranger and better? We need you! Reach out!

Early ideas (for inspiration, not restriction):

  • Techno party: A special guest who can't stop won't stop dancing (optional: music on or off).
  • The Bard: A special guest composing music about the meeting.
  • Move it out: Interpretive dance to communicate your point/pitch.
  • Art window: Original art for your meeting.
  • Hype friend: A hype-person for your meeting (or for just you).
  • Half-time show: A special musical guest for your mid-meeting break.
  • The diversion: Two guests have a special moment during your call.
  • Human timer: A guest who enforces equal speaking time for all.
  • Critic: Are you surrounded by yes people? Here is someone who will say no.
  • Finisher: A guest who makes sure the meeting ends on time - by any means necessary.

How will this work? Maybe we'll make a marketplace, with weird services for hire. Maybe people send us their links and hope we come? We'll figure exactly what this thing is and does together.

Have more ideas? Or want to bring one of these examples to life? Reach out!
