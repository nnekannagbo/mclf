Websites are great!

With your own website, you have a space that belongs to you, that you can shape to your own vision. You don't have to pay any monthly fees, and it's easy for you to update. You can showcase your work, link to other sites, and build a permanent online space where people can find you.

Join our workshop

Join our pay-what-you-can workshop for total beginners to make a one-page website and learn the fundamentals of HTML and CSS along the way.

You'll leave the workshop with:

  1. A simple one-page website (like this one) with images and external links
  2. An understanding of the fundamentals of HTML and CSS
  3. New knowledge and skills

Structure

The workshop will last 4 hours and will include 6 sections with breaks in between each sections:

  1. Introduction to HTML
  2. Making content
  3. Introduction to CSS
  4. Using a text editor (Visual Studio Code)
  5. Putting your website online (Github Pages)
  6. Overflow: questions and experimentation

Notes

  • This is a workshop for total beginners. Bring a deep love for tiny steps and thoughtful slowness.
  • This workshop won't cover: custom URLs, multipage sites, contact forms, fancy CSS, animations, JavaScript, or payment processing. All of these features are totally possible in future additional workshops, just ask!
  • If you have accessibility needs, desires, or questions, please reach out.
  • All workshop fees will go into the labour of designing and hosting this and future workshops.
  • We will take breaks between each section, this is intended to be gentle and slow.
