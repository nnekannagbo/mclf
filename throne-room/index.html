Throne Room
Online Bathroom Rituals

Throne Room is a public bathroom created for events online.

In the Throne Room you are encouraged to draw graffitti collaboratively with others and to consider things you'd like to flush away.

This project was shown at A MAZE. Berlin 2020.

Throne Room is currently "closed" but you can still visit and peek around.
