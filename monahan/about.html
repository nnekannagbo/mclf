About Monahan

A sound piece you listen to on your personal device. Experience the site anew with each visit, through dozens of sounds and stories inspired by the Monahan wetlands. Monahan is on the traditional lands of the [LAND ACKNOWLEDGMENT]

How to listen

Scan the QR code with your phone, arrive at a website, and choose the desired length of your walk. A personal sound piece PUBLIC SOUND ART is generated for you, composed from a series of chapters.

Where to find us

Visit the Monahan Drain in Ottawa, ON. You'll find QR codes that you can scan with your phone to generate a sound piece. You will need data to access the website. You can also generate a sound piece online.
