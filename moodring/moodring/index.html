Mood Ring

What do you seek?

3 questions
for you
seeker

One:
will you float up or will you dive down?

One:
do you flow out or do you curl in?

Two:
do you pull the deep flowing dark or the light floating joy?

Three:
do you await the cusp of morning or the deepening pulse of night?

Three:
does the pattern unfold or does the infinite cycle loop?

Three:
will you leap into the chaos or undulate into the gentle?

Three:
will you wander into a deep forest or wind over an endless meadow?

Find your song,
seeker?
